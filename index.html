Ty Kroll graduated from the UW Madison School of Music with an emphasis in composition. He continues to sing with the UW Choral Union, Madison Summer Choir, and has been involved more recently with the Madison Flute Club. Ty's works have been performed locally by the flute club and the Madison Men's Choir as well as internationally.
